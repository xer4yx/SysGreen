PRIVACY POLICY

In short
SysGreen collects no personal information, contains no advertising or third-party trackers, and sends no usage or telemetry data anywhere. Everything SysGreen records stays on your computer. The only time SysGreen contacts the internet is to check for and download its own updates.

What SysGreen stores on your device
To do its job and to let you undo changes, SysGreen keeps a small local database on your computer. It holds your usage history (which applications start on this PC and roughly when, used only to suggest items you may no longer need), your change records (what you have disabled, enabled, or ended, together with the prior state, so that every change can be reversed), and your preferences and corrections. This data is stored in a folder under your Windows local application data, kept outside the program's install folder so it is not lost when SysGreen updates or is uninstalled. It never leaves your computer.

What SysGreen does not do
SysGreen does not collect your name, email address, accounts, documents, or any personal identifiers. It does not include analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers. It does not transmit your usage history, change records, or settings to us or to anyone else. It has no online account and no server backend.

Network activity
The only network connection SysGreen makes is to GitHub, to check whether a newer version is available and, if you choose to install it, to download that update. These requests are served by GitHub and are subject to GitHub's own privacy practices. SysGreen does not attach any identifying information to them.

Your choices and control
Launch tracking is optional. It is on by default to power recommendations and can be turned off at any time in Settings; with it off, recommendations come only from the built-in, offline knowledge base. You can erase your data at any time using "Reset my data" in Settings. You also decide what happens on uninstall: by default your data is kept, so a reinstall continues where you left off, but you can choose to have it deleted when you uninstall.

Diagnostics
Any error or diagnostic logs SysGreen writes are saved to a local file on your computer only, and are never uploaded.

Your rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and similar laws)
Because SysGreen does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any server, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. The information SysGreen keeps lives entirely on your own device, under your control, and you can remove it yourself at any time as described above.

Children
SysGreen is a general-purpose system utility, is not directed at children, and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.
